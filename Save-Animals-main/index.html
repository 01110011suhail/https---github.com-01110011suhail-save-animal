About Us

Who We Are

At WildGuardians, we are dedicated to the preservation and protection of endangered species worldwide. Our mission is to raise awareness, provide resources for conservation efforts, and take direct action to save vulnerable wildlife from extinction. With over a decade of experience, our team of environmentalists, biologists, and volunteers works tirelessly to protect Earth's most precious creatures and their habitats.

Projects

What We Have Done

1. **Project Savannah Shield** – A dedicated effort to protect the last remaining herds of African elephants from poaching in Kenya’s savannah.

2. **Project Ocean Rescue** – Initiatives aimed at cleaning up ocean pollution and protecting marine life.

3. **Project Forest Guardians** – Reforestation efforts to restore habitats for endangered species in the Amazon rainforest.
